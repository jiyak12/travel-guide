Discover Goa

About

Goa is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage. This former Portuguese colony attracts millions of visitors each year.

Attractions

  • Baga Beach - Known for its lively atmosphere.
  • Anjuna Beach - Famous for its flea market and parties.
  • Fort Aguada - A well-preserved fort with stunning views.
  • Spice Plantations - Experience the aromatic world of spices.
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage site.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from November to February.
  • Explore both North and South Goa for different experiences.
  • Try local Goan cuisine like fish curry and vindaloo.
  • Rent a scooter for easy exploration of the area.

Accommodations

  • Taj Exotica - A luxurious beachfront resort.
  • Hotel La Calypso - Comfortable stay near Baga Beach.
  • The Lazy Frog - A charming budget hotel in Carmona.
  • W Goa - A modern luxury hotel in Vagator.
