Attractions

  • Fort Kochi: A historic area with colonial architecture and Chinese fishing nets.
  • Jew Town and Paradesi Synagogue: Showcasing Jewish history and culture.
  • Mattancherry Palace: Also called the Dutch Palace, featuring murals and artifacts.
  • Marine Drive: A scenic promenade ideal for evening strolls.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from September to March.
  • Take a backwater tour for a unique experience..
  • Explore local markets for spices and handicrafts.
