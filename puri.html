Discover Puri

About

Puri, a coastal city in Odisha, is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus and part of the Char Dham Yatra. It is renowned for the Jagannath Temple and the annual Rath Yatra festival.

Attractions

  • Jagannath Temple: Dedicated to Lord Jagannath, an important Hindu deity.
  • Puri Beach: A serene beach ideal for relaxation and sunrises.
  • Konark Sun Temple: A UNESCO World Heritage Site located nearby, famous for its intricate architecture.
  • Chilika Lake: Asiaâ€™s largest brackish water lagoon, perfect for bird watching and spotting dolphins.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to February.
  • Dress modestly while visiting temples.
  • Plan your visit during Rath Yatra for a unique cultural experience.
