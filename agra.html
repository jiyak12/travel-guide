Discover Agra

About

Agra, located in Uttar Pradesh, is synonymous with the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. This historic city was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and is renowned for its architectural marvels and rich heritage.

Attractions

  • Taj Mahal: The world-famous white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
  • Agra Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site showcasing Mughal architecture.
  • Fatehpur Sikri: A historic city built by Emperor Akbar, featuring Buland Darwaza and Jama Masjid.
  • Mehtab Bagh: A garden offering stunning views of the Taj Mahal at sunset.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to March .
  • Try Agra's famous petha (sweet) and Mughlai dishes like biryani and kebabs.
  • Use cycle rickshaws or hire a guide for exploring monuments.
  • Visit Sadar Bazaar for handicrafts and leather goods.

Accommodations

  • The Oberoi Amarvilas: A luxury hotel with views of the Taj Mahal.
  • ITC Mughal: Offers a royal experience with modern amenities.
  • Hotel Taj Resorts: A mid-range option close to the Taj Mahal.
  • Zostel Agra: A budget-friendly hostel for backpackers.
