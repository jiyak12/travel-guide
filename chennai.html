Discover Chennai

About

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is known for its cultural heritage, classical music, dance forms, and beautiful beaches. Itâ€™s a bustling metropolis that showcases a blend of tradition and modernity.

Attractions

  • Marina Beach: One of the longest urban beaches in the world, perfect for evening strolls.
  • Fort St. George: The first fortress built by the British in India, now housing a museum.
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva with stunning Dravidian architecture.
  • Government Museum: Showcases a vast collection of art and archaeological artifacts.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from November to February.
  • Explore local cuisine; don't miss out on dosa and filter coffee.
  • Use public transport or ride-sharing apps to navigate the city easily.

Accommodations

  • Taj Coromandel: A luxury hotel located in the heart of the city with excellent amenities.
  • The Leela Palace: Offers opulent accommodations and fine dining experiences.
  • Radisson Blu Hotel Chennai City Centre: Centrally located with modern facilities.
  • Upar Hotels in T. Nagar: Affordable options close to shopping areas.
