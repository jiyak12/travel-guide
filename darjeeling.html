Discover Darjeeling

About

Darjeeling is renowned for its tea plantations, stunning views of the Himalayas, and colonial charm. Itâ€™s often referred to as the "Queen of Hills" due to its picturesque landscapes and pleasant climate.

Attractions

  • Tiger Hill: Famous for its sunrise views over Kanchenjunga, the third highest mountain in the world.
  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train): A UNESCO World Heritage site offering scenic rides through tea gardens.
  • Batasia Loop: A unique railway loop that provides panoramic views of Darjeeling town and mountains.
  • Peace Pagoda: A beautiful stupa showcasing Buddhist teachings.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from March to June or September to November when weather conditions are ideal.
  • Try local delicacies like momos (dumplings) and Darjeeling tea during your stay.
  • Be prepared for hilly terrain; comfortable shoes are recommended.

Accommodations

  • Windamere Hotel: A historic hotel offering colonial charm and beautiful views of the mountains.
  • Mayfair Darjeeling: A luxury resort set amidst lush gardens with top-notch amenities.
  • Hotel Sonar Bangla Darjeeling: Offers comfortable stays with great views at reasonable prices.
