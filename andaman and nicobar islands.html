Discover Andaman and Nicobar Islands

About

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a group of islands in the Bay of Bengal known for their pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life.

Attractions

  • Radhanagar Beach: One of Asia’s best beaches, located on Havelock Island.
  • Cellular Jail: A historic site showcasing India’s freedom struggle.
  • Ross Island: A former British administrative headquarters with ruins.
  • Baratang Island: Known for its limestone caves and mangroves.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from November to May..
  • Book ferry and activity tickets in advance during peak season.
  • Avoid the monsoon season as activities are limited.

Accommodations

  • Luxury: Taj Exotica Resort & Spa, Havelock Island Beach Resort.
  • Mid-range: Symphony Samudra, Seashell Havelock.
  • Budget: Barefoot at Havelock, Andaman Pearl.
