Discover Bangalore

About

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is India's Silicon Valley due to its thriving IT industry. The city combines modernity with greenery, offering pleasant weather year-round along with cultural landmarks.

Attractions

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden: Known for its glasshouse and rare plant species.
  • Bangalore Palace: Inspired by England's Windsor Castle, it showcases Tudor-style architecture.
  • Cubbon Park: A sprawling green space ideal for relaxation amidst nature.
  • Vidhana Soudha: The seat of Karnataka's legislature with impressive architecture.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to February.
  • Savor South Indian dishes like dosa, idli, vada, and filter coffee at local eateries.
  • Use metro services or app-based cabs for hassle-free travel; avoid peak traffic hours.
  • Visit Commercial Street and Brigade Road for trendy apparel and souvenirs.

Accommodations

  • The Leela Palace Bengaluru: A luxurious hotel with world-class amenities.
  • ITC Gardenia Bengaluru: Known for eco-friendly luxury accommodations.
  • Treebo Trend Hotel Edge St Marks Road: Mid-range option near shopping hubs.
  • Social Rehab Hostel Koramangala: Budget-friendly accommodation for backpackers.
