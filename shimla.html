Discover Shimla

About

Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and is known for its colonial architecture and scenic views. It is a popular hill station that attracts tourists year-round.

Attractions

  • The Ridge - A large open space offering stunning views.
  • Jakhoo Temple - Famous for its Hanuman statue.
  • Christ Church - The second oldest church in North India.
  • Mall Road - A bustling street with shops and eateries.
  • Kufri - Known for adventure sports and horse riding.

Travel Tips
