Discover Ladakh

About

Ladakh, known as "The Land of High Passes," is famous for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and vibrant culture. It offers breathtaking views of mountains and valleys, making it a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Attractions

  • Pangong Lake: A beautiful high-altitude lake known for its changing colors.
  • Nubra Valley: Famous for its sand dunes and Bactrian camels.
  • Thiksey Monastery: An impressive monastery resembling the Potala Palace in Lhasa.
  • Khardung La Pass: One of the highest motorable roads in the world.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from May to September.
  • Acclimatize properly due to high altitude; stay hydrated.
  • Carry cash as ATMs may not be readily available in remote areas.

Accommodations

  • The Grand Dragon Hotel: A luxury hotel offering stunning views and modern amenities.
  • Hotel Hilltop Leh: Comfortable accommodation with traditional Ladakhi hospitality.
  • Zostel Leh: Budget-friendly hostel ideal for backpackers and solo travelers.
