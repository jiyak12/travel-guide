Discover Hyderabad

About

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, diverse culture, and culinary delights. Famous for its historical landmarks and modern IT hubs, it beautifully blends tradition with contemporary life.

Attractions

  • Hussain Sagar Lake: A large artificial lake featuring a massive Buddha statue in the center.
  • Golconda Fort: A historic fort known for its impressive architecture and acoustics.
  • Charminar: An iconic symbol of the city, this 16th-century monument is surrounded by bustling markets.
  • Ramoji Film City: One of the largest film cities in the world, offering tours and entertainment.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to March.
  • Don't miss trying local dishes like Hyderabadi Biryani and Haleem.
  • Use local transport options like auto-rickshaws or app-based cabs for convenience.
  • Be cautious of traffic during peak hours as it can get congested.

Accommodations

  • Taj Falaknuma Palace: A luxurious heritage hotel with stunning views and royal hospitality.
  • ITC Kakatiya: A five-star hotel offering modern amenities and excellent dining options.
  • Radisson Blu Plaza Hotel: Conveniently located with comfortable rooms and facilities.
  • Treebo Trend: Budget-friendly options available throughout the city.
