Discover Kolkata

About

Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, is a city that blends colonial charm with vibrant traditions. Known as the "City of Joy," it is famous for its literature, art, festivals, and culinary delights.

Attractions

  • Victoria Memorial: A grand marble structure dedicated to Queen Victoria.
  • Howrah Bridge: An iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River.
  • Dakshineswar Kali Temple: A revered Hindu temple on the banks of the Ganges River.
  • Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on Elephanta Island.
  • Indian Museum: The oldest museum in India with an extensive collection of artifacts.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to February for cooler weather and Durga Puja festivities in October.
  • Try Bengali sweets like rasgulla and sandesh, along with street food like kathi rolls.
  • Use yellow taxis or metro services for convenient travel.
  • Explore New Market and Gariahat Market for local textiles and crafts.

Accommodations

  • The Oberoi Grand Kolkata: A luxury hotel with colonial charm.
  • Taj Bengal Kolkata: Offers premium amenities and excellent service.
  • Hotel Park Prime Kolkata: A mid-range option near major attractions.
  • Backpackers Park Kolkata: Ideal for budget travelers.
