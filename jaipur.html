Discover the Pink City

About

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City due to the distinct color of its buildings. It is rich in history, culture, and stunning architecture.

Attractions

  • Hawa Mahal - The Palace of Winds
  • Amber Fort - A majestic fort with artistic Hindu architecture
  • City Palace - A grand complex showcasing royal heritage
  • Jantar Mantar - An astronomical observatory with impressive instruments

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to March.
  • Try traditional Rajasthani dishes like Laal Maas and Dal Baati Churma.
  • Use local transport options like auto-rickshaws for convenience.

Accommodations

  • Alsisar Haveli - A heritage hotel with traditional decor.
  • Hotel Jaipur Central - Centrally located with modern amenities.
  • Samode Haveli - An exquisite heritage property combining luxury with tradition.
