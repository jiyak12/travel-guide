Discover Varanasi

About

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Benaras, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. It holds a central place in Hindu traditions and is known for its spiritual significance along the Ganges River.

Attractions

  • Manikarnika Ghat - The main ghat for Hindu funerary rites.
  • Dashashwamedh Ghat - Famous for its evening Ganges Aarti ceremony.
  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple - A significant temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Sarnath - The site where Buddha delivered his first sermon.
  • Banaras Hindu University - One of the oldest universities in India.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit is from October to March when the weather is pleasant.
  • Experience local street food like kachori-sabzi and tamatar chaat.
  • Take a boat ride on the Ganges for a unique perspective of the ghats.
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and ghats.

Accommodations

  • Taj Ganges - A luxurious hotel with modern amenities and beautiful views.
  • Hotel Surya - A comfortable stay with traditional hospitality.
  • BrijRama Palace - A heritage hotel offering a royal experience on the ghats.
  • Ganges View Hotel - Offers stunning views of the river and easy access to major attractions.
